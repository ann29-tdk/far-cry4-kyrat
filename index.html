Why visit Kyrat?

Mountains

Kyrat is rightly called the 'Land of the Dawn-Lit Mountains'. The country boasts of several beautiful mountains offering jaw-dropping views. So, if you're planning a trip to the country, make sure you get a glimpse of these breathtaking and must-visit mountains in Kyrat while you're there for your next holiday.

Tigers

White Tigers are the most magnificent of all the cats. It is one of the five 'big cats'. A large male tiger averages about 9 feet (3mt.) in length (from head to the end of the tail).

Crab Rangoons !

Often credited with being created in the southeast Asian region previously known as Burma where Rangoon was the capital and a major city. Crab Rangoon has become a popular fried food. Typically served as an appetizer or as a main dish, Crab Rangoon is made with a combination of crab meat, cream cheese, onions, soy sauce, garlic, and seasonings that are mixed together as a filling to be enclosed within a won ton wrapper.

"World of Beautiful valley"

Kyrat is home to unexplored passes, picturesque mountains, remarkable monasteries, peaceful lakes, and many more. Varshakot is one of the Seven Federal Subjects of Kyrat and is located on the Northeastern tip of Banapur. It is beautifully surrounded by Chal Jama Monastery, Baghadur and Shanath and is blessed with an array of flora and fauna. Sounds like a paradise, right? Well, imagine being in this beautiful land.
